LOUISIANA TRAFFIC NET PREAMBLE

  • ……………Net Stand By ………….

  • Calling the Louisiana Traffic Net…………calling the Louisiana Traffic Net. This is __( your call )___ and my name is ______________ located in __________________ and I will be your net control for this session.

  • This net meets 7 days a week at 6:00 p.m. Central Time on or about the frequency of 3.910 kilohertz for the purpose of handling all traffic. Our alternate frequencies are 3.913 or 3.907 depending on QRM or when the need arises.

  • This is a directed net so please transmit only when directed to do so by Net Control EXCEPT in the case of an Emergency.

  • If you have an Emergency, use the double break and identify your station. The net will then stand by to assist you with your immediate problem. Relaying stations should say “ Relay “ , state your call sign and wait for Net Control to recognize you. List all other traffic when directed to do so by Net Control.

  • Are there any stations with Emergency, Medical, Priority or Time Valued traffic at this time, please call.

  • Hearing none, would anyone like to lead us in the Pledge of Allegiance tonight? If not, please stand if you can and place your hand over your heart and join me in the Pledge……………

  • I pledge allegiance to the Flag of the United States of America, and to the Republic for which it stands, one Nation under God, indivisible, with liberty and justice for all.

  • Would anyone like to offer a short prayer tonight? Hearing none, please bow your heads………….. ( you have the prayer )

  • (Thank the person for the Pledge and/or Prayer). Are there any stations with traffic tolist? Are there any low power or mobile stations? Any relays? Are there any stations that went to Cycle 4 CW Net for the region that has not checked in ?

  • I would like to recognize early check-ins as I have them. If I miss anyone please break and let me know………. { call check-ins on your roll }

  • Are there any additional stations with or without traffic needing to check in before we go to any announcements?

  • Hearing no more, I will now turn the net over to Jim, for any announcements, and anyone after that. (recognize anyone with an announcement or quote or note )

  • After all announcements, ask if there are any additional check-ins…… If none, I want to Thank everyone for joining and checking into our net tonight. We invite you back whenever you can check in as you are always welcome.

  • Release stations – either one at a time or the entire net depending on band conditions. ( When done ) Jim, AB5YS are you ready to copy my net report? (Include your message number, number of check ins, number of pieces of traffic INCLUDING our net report, and time of net.) (Then release Jim.)

  • Thank everyone again for coming…… and this is _____(your call) ______ closing this session of the Louisiana Traffic Net returning the frequency back to regular amateur radio use. 73’s….
